# Budget and Limits Policy

The local ledger is a guardrail, not a billing source of truth.

Track:
- estimated tokens;
- premium calls;
- steering comments;
- session count;
- route choice;
- failures;
- provider and role.

Real balances must be verified through official provider usage/billing surfaces or trusted exports.

Default hard stops:
- overage disabled unless explicitly allowed;
- hard stop at 90% local budget;
- preserve a skill after repeated failure;
- reroute if Copilot steering comments exceed the planned limit.
